Behind the lens

For those interested in the man behind the camera... This handsome dude is me, Tim Clough. I'm originally from South Africa but have been happily living in Bristol, England for the last decade. If you're in the city and you see some guy busking in the park drinking Guiness, wearing a Daniel Johnston t-shirt and playing Elliott Smith tunes on his guitar, it's probably me. Come say hey.
